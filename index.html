FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What therapy areas does Dominus Healthcare focus on?

Six: diabetes, cardiovascular, nutrition & wellness, gastro, CNS and dermatology — with 20+ brands concentrated where the disease burden in India is heaviest.

Where are Dominus products manufactured?

Exclusively at WHO-GMP and ISO certified partner facilities under Dominus quality oversight, with three independent checkpoints: raw material, in-process and finished product.

How can I become a marketing or PCD franchise partner?

We appoint regional marketing partners across India by state or district. Visit the Partner page and submit an enquiry — our business development team replies within two working days.

Are the products available on prescription only?

Most brands are prescription (Schedule H) medicines for registered medical practitioners. Nutraceuticals (Nuproten-D, Sunify 60K, Lycop MM, Nuvinus-4G) and Nufeet are supplements / OTC.

How do I report an adverse event?

Contact pv@dominushealthcare.com or call +91-8277579999. Reports are handled by our pharmacovigilance team.
